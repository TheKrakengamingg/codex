Use this skill for marketing content strategy, Shorts scripts, ElevenLabs scripts, SEO keywords in transcripts, YouTube descriptions, CTA design, and repurposing content into web/Discord/social posts.

Principles:
- A video is often the first contact; it must lead somewhere useful.
- Scripts should include natural SEO phrases without sounding robotic.
- The transcript should reinforce target keywords and brand terms.
- Every content asset should map to a funnel step: awareness, interest, join, apply, donate, return.

Shorts lifecycle:
1. Choose topic/hook.
2. Generate script with natural target keywords.
3. Generate title, description, hashtags, and transcript.
4. Add CTA linking to relevant landing page or Discord.
5. Repurpose into website article/FAQ/Discord post when useful.
6. Track performance and feed learnings into future hooks.

CTA examples:
- `Join OG-RP today`
- `Apply for whitelist`
- `Watch the full update on krakencore.org`
- `Join the Discord before the next event`

Quality rules:
- Do not keyword-stuff.
- Do not overpromise.
- Keep ElevenLabs speech natural and varied.
- Align visual prompts with the target landing page or community identity.
