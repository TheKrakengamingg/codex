Use this skill for competitor research, market scans, trend reports, FiveM server analysis, gaming community benchmarking, and identifying features OG-RP can do better.

Research targets:
- FiveM/RP server websites.
- Discord onboarding and community flows.
- YouTube/TikTok/Shorts content strategies.
- Donation/store structures.
- Feature announcements and changelogs.
- New technology adoption: C#, Helix-like systems, inventory frameworks, anti-cheat, queue systems, live overlays.

Analysis rules:
- Do not copy blindly. Extract the principle, then propose a better OG-RP version.
- Separate proven trends from one-off gimmicks.
- Look for weak spots competitors have: confusing onboarding, slow sites, poor mobile UX, unclear rules, weak content.
- Prioritize early-mover opportunities with realistic implementation cost.

Report format:
- Trend observed.
- Evidence/source.
- Why it matters.
- How OG-RP can do it better.
- Effort level: low/medium/high.
- Risk or dependency.

If browsing is needed for current competitor data, use current sources and include links.
